Friday Night Shop Hop

#troyshophop #downtowntroy #homegrownvibrant #shopsmall #buylocal


The Friday Night Shop is a downtown Troy merchant driven shopping and dining event modeled after the Gallery Hop in Columbus, Ohioâ€™s Short North Arts District. The Shop Hop begins at 5:00pm and runs until 9:00pm on the last Friday of every month. Visitors enjoy shopping, dining, special events, arts activities, and street performers throughout the evening.


I serve as the lead Graphic Designer for the Friday Night Shop Hop creating all print and digital files, including posters, mini-flyers and social media imagery. I drew inspiration for the April / May / June Shop Hop color palette from the sugar-laced leftover Easter peeps I loving ate as I designed the first poster. The second poster, designed in the heat of an exceptional busy event season at Troy Main Street, reminds me of the circus: bright and colorful with a little touch of crazy.


During the Shop Hop, I serve as the event photographer and Instagramer.


Client: Troy Main Street
