Piqua Fine Art Exhibition logo


The Piqua Fine Art Exhibition is a an art show and sale hosted by the Piqua Arts Council. The 2016 exhibit featured over 200 pieces of art. Monies raised from event contribute to the organization's mission of making the arts accessible to the community through education, support and presentation.


Inspiration for the design of the fine art exhibition logo comes from a fusion of history and art. The quill conveys the beauty found in fine art, whereas the colorful feather represents Piqua's Native America history.


Client: Piqua Arts Council
