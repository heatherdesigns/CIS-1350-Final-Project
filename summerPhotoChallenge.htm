Summer Photo Challenge

@visitmiamico #miamicophoto #homegrowngreat


Miami County celebrated National Tourism Week, May 1st through May 7th, with the launch of the 2016 Summer Photo Challenge. From May 1st until July 31st, residents and visitors are challenged to post images of their favorite foods, drinks & sweet treats to Instagram.


Participants can earn a free t-shirt, bag and entry into the grand prize drawing.


I served as the lead Graphic Designer for the Summer Photo Challenge creating the rack card and t-shirt shown.


Client: Miami County Visitors & Convention Bureau
