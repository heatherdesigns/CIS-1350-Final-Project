Downtown Troy Farmers Market

Home. Grown. Eats.


The Downtown Troy Farmers Market is open Saturdayâ€™s, 9am to Noon, from June through September. The market features 25 to 30 growers, producers, artists and craftsmen.


I served as the lead Graphic Designer for the Downtown Troy Farmers Market creating all print and digital files, including the 2015 poster series, t-shirts, and business cards as shown.


I will also take credit for the marketing genius behind the tagline line, Home. Grown. Eats. In 2013, Miami County repositioned their marketing efforts using the strapline Home. Grown. Great. Community organizations were encouraged to adopt and adapt the strapline to an effort to expand a cohesive County branding. Therefore, Home. Grown. Eats. was born.


I also continue to serve as the market photographer and Instagramer.
