Heather Taylor

Graphic Designer / Web Developer


Hello! Thank you for stopping by. Since you’re here, let’s talk about me. I am a Graphic Designer that has taken the plunge into the world of web development. Exactly one year ago I began my journey with an ‘Introduction to Web Development’ course (aka: C++) at a local community college. I have a long laundry list of languages I would like to learn. At this time I have conquered classes in C++ and HTML/CSS. Next on my agenda for the fall of 2015 is a course in database management.


I am a uniquely balanced right brain / left brain person. The colorful, intuitive, creative side of me finds expression in my work as a graphic designer. The logical, organized, analytical side of me has found an outlet in web development.


As the Graphic Designer for two non-profit organizations, my design work is geared towards the event and destination marketing. Beautiful posters, rack cards, annual reports, logos, magazine ads and the occasional super-cool t-shirt design fill my days.


As a budding Web Developer, I seek to learn more! Writing clean code is just as important to me as creating visually appealing design. Follow my work on GitHub as I continue to learn and grow.
