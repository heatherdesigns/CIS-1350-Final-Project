Heather Taylor

Graphic Designer / Web Developer


Hello! Thank you for stopping by. Since you’re here, let’s talk about me. I am a Graphic Designer that has taken the plunge into the world of web development. Almost two years ago I began my journey with an ‘Introduction to Web Development’ course (aka: C++) at a local community college. I've discovered there is so much to learn! At this time I have conquered classes in C++, HTML & CSS, Database Management, and ASP.NET in C#. I am currently enrolled in an Introduciton to Java class. I am also an active member of Girl Develop It - Dayton and have taken workshops in HTML & CSS, Ruby, and JavaScript.


I am a uniquely balanced right brain / left brain person. The colorful, intuitive, creative side of me finds expression in my work as a graphic designer. The logical, organized, analytical side of me has found an outlet in web development.


As the Graphic Designer for a local visitors bureau, my design work is geared towards promoting tourisum to our beautiful county. Posters, banners, rack cards, logos, magazine ads and the occasional super-cool t-shirt design fill my days.


As an aspiring Web Developer, I seek to learn more! Writing clean code is just as important to me as creating visually appealing designs. Follow my work on GitHub as I continue to learn and grow.
